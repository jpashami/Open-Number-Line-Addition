How to Play

Addition Mode

Start at a number and add jumps of +10 or +1 to reach the target sum.

Grouping Magic ✨

Make 10 little jumps (+1) in a row, and watch them combine into one big jump (+10)!

Swap Mode

Click "Swap" to flip the numbers (e.g., 25+30 becomes 30+25).

Subtraction Mode

Start at a bigger number and jump backwards (-10 or -1) to reach the target.

Custom Problems

Click the ✎ pencil icon next to the problem to enter your own numbers!
